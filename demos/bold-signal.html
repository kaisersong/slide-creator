How It Works

From idea to presentation in minutes

01
/slide-creator
Answer 4 questions: purpose, length, content, images. That's the whole intake.
02
Pick your style
React to 3 visual previews. No describing design in words. Just point at the one you want.
03
Present it
Arrow keys. Space. Swipe. Click dots. Your presentation runs in any browser, forever.
06 06 / 08
Key Features

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Viewport fitting
Every slide fits exactly 100vh. No scrolling ever. Looks identical on every screen.
Inline editing
Press E, click any text, edit in place. Ctrl+S saves. No code. No IDE.
PPTX export
/slide-creator --export pptx via Playwright + your Chrome. Real slides, real format.
Two-stage workflow
--plan creates the outline first. --generate produces the HTML. Review before you render.

Installation

Claude Code

OpenClaw
