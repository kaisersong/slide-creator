問
The Condition

"In the noise of technology,
clarity is scarce."

Distraction

Every tool adds layers. Animations, effects, transitions — the medium overwhelms the message. What remains is noise, not signal.

Uniformity

Ten thousand slides dressed in the same template. Sans-serif, bright colors, motion everywhere. No voice. No distinction. Forgettable by design.

Dependence

Locked ecosystems. Files held hostage. One company's terms govern your words. A presentation should be yours alone to keep and share.

Method

Three steps.
One complete work.

First
/slide-creator

Invoke the command. Answer the questions. No configuration, no setup, no hidden complexity.

Second
Choose your style

Three visual paths appear before you. React with your senses, not with theory. The body knows what words cannot say.

Third
Present with grace

Pure HTML, immediately ready. Arrow keys. Space. Touch. No runtime, no cloud, no external dependency.

能
Capabilities

Built for the
working moment.

Full Height

Each slide fills 100vh exactly. No scrolling, no waste — every layout respects the screen as a complete space.

Inline Edit

Press E, click any text, Ctrl+S to save. Edit mode lives within every presentation. Revision becomes seamless.

PowerPoint Export

/slide-creator --export pptx transforms to PowerPoint when institutional demands require it. Your HTML never breaks.

Two-Stage Workflow

--plan creates the outline. --generate produces final HTML. Review before commitment. Intention precedes execution.
